Boston Package

Day 1: Boston arrival
Get to Logan International Airport (BOS) first. Private transportation to your accommodation in the heart of Boston. Free evening to see neighboring sites like as Faneuil Hall or Quincy Market.

Day 2: City Tour of Historic Boston
Tour with a guide of Boston's historical sites: The Trail of Freedom Massachusetts State House in Boston Common The Home of Paul Revere A visit to the Boston Tea Party Ships and Museum in the afternoon.

Day 3: Tour of Harvard and MIT
A morning trip to Cambridge for MIT and Harvard University guided tours. In the afternoon, take a stroll along the Charles River Esplanade. Optional: Take a Boston Harbor evening cruise for breathtaking views of the city skyline.

Day 4: Visit to Cape Cod or Salem for the day
Select from: Salem: Take in the historic streets and the Salem Witch Museum. Cape Cod: Unwind on stunning beaches and explore quaint seaside communities.

Day 5: Boston's Food and Culture
A morning trip to the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum or the Museum of Fine Arts. Free day for shopping in Copley Place or Newbury Street. Evening: Savor a traditional seafood meal that includes lobster rolls or Boston's renowned clam chowder.

Day 6: Whale Watching or the Boston Harbor Islands
Hike and take in the beautiful scenery while touring the Boston Harbor Islands for the day. Optional: Take an exhilarating Atlantic Ocean whale-watching excursion.

Day 7: Leaving
Check-out and breakfast. Last-minute sightseeing or shopping prior to your flight.

Miami Package

Explore the Wonderful City! Prepare to enjoy the sun, exciting nightlife, and the charm of Miami. Ideal for families, couples, and single adventurers.

What's included?

The accommodation:
Stay at a magnificent seaside resort or a trendy downtown hotel (3, 4, or 5-star ratings available). Complimentary breakfast and pool access.

The Transportation:
Round-trip airfare from major cities. Airport pick-up and drop-off services

Exclusive Perks:
Complimentary cocktail on arrival. Discounts on Miami nightlife experiences.

Day 1: Arrival in Miami
Welcome drink at a beachfront hotel. Relax by the pool or explore South Beach.

Day 2: Miami City Tour Visit iconic landmarks like Little Havana, Wynwood Walls, and the Art Deco District. Enjoy a Cuban-inspired lunch in Little Havana.

Day 3: Biscayne Bay Adventure Morning: Jet skiing or kayaking in Biscayne Bay. Evening: Sunset boat cruise with refreshments.

Day 4: Free Day for Exploration Optional activities: Snorkeling, Everglades Airboat Tour, or shopping at Bayside Marketplace.

Day 5: Key West Day Trip Full-day guided tour to Key West via the scenic Overseas Highway. Visit Ernest Hemingway’s Home, and enjoy the lively Duval Street.

Day 6: Nightlife and Relaxation Relax at the beach during the day. Experience Miami’s vibrant nightlife with a VIP club entry (optional).

Day 7: Departure Breakfast and a relaxed checkout. Airport drop-off services.

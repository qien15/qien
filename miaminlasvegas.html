Miami and Las Vegas Package

Day 1: Miami arrival
Greetings from Miami! Private transportation to your accommodation on the beach. Enjoy a free evening to unwind or explore South Beach.

Day 2: Tour of Miami City
guided tour of Miami that includes the Art Deco District, Wynwood Walls, and Little Havana. Enjoy Miami's nightlife for a free evening.

Day 3: Adventure in Biscayne Bay
Paddleboard or kayak on Biscayne Bay in the morning. Evening: Free beverages and a sunset cruise.

Day 4: Day Trip to the Everglades and Key West
Airboat cruise in the Everglades for the entire day. Take the picturesque Overseas Highway to Key West.

Day 5: Unwind and Take Off for Las Vegas
Enjoy a leisurely morning on Miami's beaches. fly to Las Vegas in the afternoon. Free to tour the Las Vegas Strip in the evening.

Day 6: City Tour of Las Vegas
Discover famous sites including The Venetian, Fremont Street, and the Bellagio Fountains. Gondola trip at The Venetian is optional.

Day 7: Tour of the Grand Canyon and Hoover Dam
tour of the Grand Canyon (West or South Rim) with a guide for the entire day. Visit Hoover Dam for sweeping vistas.

Day 8: Nightlife & Adventure
Morning: Helicopter excursion above the Strip is optional. Evening: VIP access to the best nightclubs in Las Vegas.

Day 9: leisure and shopping
Free morning to browse at upscale stores. Enjoy a spa day or unwind by the pool.

Day 10: Vegas Free Day
Red Rock is an optional activity.

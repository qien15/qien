Los Angles

Explore the glitz and glamour of Las Vegas with thrilling attractions, exquisite lodgings, and amazing experiences.

Day 1: Arrival in Las Vegas
Arrival at Las Vegas McCarran International Airport. Check-in at a luxurious hotel on the Las Vegas Strip. Evening free to explore the iconic casinos and dazzling lights.

Day 2: Las Vegas City Tour
Morning: Guided city tour covering landmarks like the Bellagio Fountains, The Venetian, Fremont Street, and the High Roller Observation Wheel. Afternoon: Free time for shopping at the Fashion Show Mall or premium outlets. Evening: Enjoy a world-class Vegas show (e.g., Cirque du Soleil).

Day 3: Grand Canyon & Hoover Dam Day Trip
Full-day guided tour to the Grand Canyon (West Rim or South Rim). Stop at the iconic Hoover Dam and take in breathtaking views. Enjoy a picnic lunch during the tour.

Day 4: Adventure and Nightlife.
Morning: Optional activities include skydiving, a helicopter tour of the Strip, and a spa day. Afternoon: Relax at your resort's pool. Evening: Nightclub crawl with VIP access to Las Vegas' premier clubs.

Day 5: Departure
Breakfast and checkout. At the last minute shopping or casino entertainment before the airport transfer.

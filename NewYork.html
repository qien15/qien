New York Package

Explore New York City's renowned monuments, bustling neighborhoods, and unequaled vitality. From world-famous sights to hidden jewels, this extended excursion will provide you with an authentic Big Apple experience!

Day 1: arrival in New York City.
Arrive at either Newark (EWR) or John F. Kennedy (JFK) International Airport. Private transportation to your premium hotel in Manhattan. Evening free for relaxation or a trip through Times Square.

Day 2: Manhattan Exploration
Morning guided tour of Midtown Manhattan, including Rockefeller Center, Grand Central Terminal, and the magnificent Empire State Building. Afternoon: Visit the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA). Evening: Dinner at a prestigious Manhattan restaurant.

Day 3: Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.
A ferry voyage to Liberty Island provides a close-up glimpse of the Statue of Liberty. Tour Ellis Island to learn about America's immigrant past. Optional: Tour Battery Park or the Financial District.

Day 4: Central Park & Upper Manhattan
Spend the morning exploring Central Park on foot, bike, or carriage. Visit either the American Museum of Natural History or the Guggenheim Museum. Free evening to shop on 5th Avenue or relax.

Day 5: Brooklyn Day Trip.
A guided tour of Brooklyn, including DUMBO, Brooklyn Bridge Park, and Williamsburg. Enjoy breathtaking views of Manhattan from the Brooklyn Heights Promenade. Taste local specialties at Smorgasburg (a seasonal food market).

Day 6: Broadway & Nightlife
A free morning to explore or shop. The afternoon and evening are devoted for a Broadway musical (tickets included). Optional: VIP club access allows you to explore NYC's lively evening scene.

Day 7: Harlem, Cultural Immersion
Morning walking tour of Harlem, including the Apollo Theater and historical sites. Optional: attend a live gospel service. Evening free to pursue further cultural activities.

Day 8: Day Trip to the Hudson Valley
Full-day excursion to the picturesque Hudson Valley. Visit historic estates, wineries, or take a nature hike. Return to NYC in the evening for some leisure time.

Day 9: Departure
Breakfast and hotel check-out. Last-minute shopping or sightseeing before your airport transfer.

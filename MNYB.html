Miami & New York & Boston Package

The plan of the Trip

Day 1: Miami arrival
At Miami International Airport, arrive. Private transportation to your accommodation on the beach. Free to explore South Beach in the evening.

Day 2: Tour of Miami City
guided tour of Miami's main attractions, including the Art Deco District, Wynwood Walls, and Little Havana.

Day 3: Adventure in Biscayne Bay
Paddleboarding or kayaking on Biscayne Bay in the morning. free beers on a sunset cruise.

Day 4: Adventure in the Everglades
Take an airboat trip across the Everglades to observe animals and alligators.

Day 5: Key West Day Trip
A picturesque journey on the Overseas Highway. Discover the beaches and historic landmarks of Key West.

Day 6: Miami Free Day
Shopping, spa services, or lounging on the beach are optional activities. Flight to New York City on Day 7 flight to New York City in the morning. Private transportation to your accommodation in Manhattan. Nine Days, Eight Nights in New York City

Day 8: Getting There and Seeing the City
You may spend the evening exploring Times Square and the surrounding sights.

Day 9: Tour of Midtown Manhattan
guided tour of famous sites such as Grand Central Terminal, Rockefeller Center, and the Empire State Building.

Day 10: Ellis Island and the Statue of Liberty
For stunning views of the Statue of Liberty, take a ferry to Liberty Island.

Day 11: Upper Manhattan & Central Park
Visit the American Museum of Natural History after taking a stroll or a bike ride in Central Park.

Day 12: Day Trip to Brooklyn
Visit Brooklyn Bridge Park, Williamsburg, and DUMBO. Free day to explore or shop. In the evening, take in a Broadway performance (tickets included).

Day 13:Free Time

Day 14: Hudson Valley Day Trip
Go hiking, see ancient estates, or visit picturesque wineries.

Day 15: NYC Free Day
Shopping, a helicopter trip, or a visit to the One World Observatory and 9/11 Memorial are optional options.
Day 16: Travel to Boston via train

A beautiful train journey to Boston in the morning. Five Days, Five Nights in Boston
Day 17: Boston arrival
You may spend the evening exploring the Seaport District or Quincy Market.

Day 18: Tour of Historic Boston
Discover Paul Revere's House, Boston Common, and the Freedom Trail.

Day 19: MIT & Harvard
guided tour of Cambridge's MIT and Harvard. Boston Harbor evening cruise (optional).

Day 20: Leaving
Check-out and breakfast. For your journey home, a private airport shuttle is required.

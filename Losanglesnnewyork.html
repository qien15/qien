Los Angles and New York Packages

Day 1: Los Angeles arrival
Greetings from LA! Private transportation to your opulent hotel in Downtown Los Angeles or Beverly Hills. Free evening to unwind or see nearby attractions.

Day 2: Tour of Hollywood and Beverly Hills
Morning: A guided tour of the Hollywood Sign, the TCL Chinese Theatre, and the Walk of Fame, among other famous sites. In the afternoon, go shopping and take in the sights at Rodeo Drive and Beverly Hills.

Day 3: Venice Beach and Santa Monica
Explore Venice Beach and Santa Monica Pier in the morning. Bicycle rides around the shoreline are optional in the afternoon. Enjoy a free evening to unwind or eat at a restaurant by the water.

Day 4: Hollywood's Universal Studios
admission to Universal Studios Hollywood for the entire day. Savor exhilarating thrills, cinematic magic behind the scenes, and entertainment in CityWalk.

Day 5: Visit to Anaheim or Malibu for the day
Exploration of the Malibu shore or a day excursion to Disneyland are optional. Free evening for shopping or recreation.

Day 6: Take a plane to NYC
fly to New York in the morning. Private transportation to your accommodation in Manhattan. Enjoy Times Square or neighboring attractions for free in the evening.

Day 7: Tour of New York City
Morning: Take in the sights of Midtown, including the Grand Central Terminal, the Empire State Building, and Rockefeller Center. Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in the afternoon.

Day 8: Ellis Island and the Statue of Liberty
For stunning views of the Statue of Liberty, take a boat to Liberty Island. Discover the history of immigrants in America by taking a tour of Ellis Island.

Day 9: Upper Manhattan & Central Park
Morning: Explore Central Park on foot or by bicycle. In the afternoon, go to the Guggenheim Museum or the American Museum of Natural History.

Day 10: Exploration of Brooklyn
Explore Williamsburg, Brooklyn Heights, and DUMBO after crossing the Brooklyn Bridge. Free evenings for leisure by the waterfront or nightlife.

Day 11: Experiencing Broadway
Free morning for relaxation or shopping. Evening: Take in a top-notch Broadway production (tickets provided).

Day 12: Hudson Valley Day Trip
A full-day trip to the Hudson Valley to see historic estates, hike, or sample wine. Goodbye meal in the evening at a renowned restaurant in Manhattan.

Day 13: NYC Free Day
Optional activities include a shopping spree, a helicopter trip, or a visit to the One World Observatory and 9/11 Memorial.

Day 14: Leaving
Check-out and breakfast. For your trip home, a private transfer will take you to the airport.
